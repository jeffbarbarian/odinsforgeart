Tattoo Apprentice

Linework first. Everything else follows.

Jeff is a tattoo apprentice working toward a style built on clean linework, Nordic knotwork, and blackwork — with American traditional and color as the long-term goal. Every session is about doing the fundamentals right before chasing the flashy stuff.

Currently taking linework-focused bookings while drilling the craft under mentorship. Clients are welcome to return later for shading or color work.
