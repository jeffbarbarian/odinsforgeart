How Booking Works

  1. Reach out

    Send your idea, reference images, rough size, and placement through any of the options above.

  2. Free consult

    We talk through design direction, sizing, and realistic expectations — in person or over message.

  3. Deposit & scheduling

    A non-refundable deposit secures your spot and goes toward the final cost of your piece.

  4. Design day

    Final design is confirmed before we start — you'll see the stencil before any ink touches skin.

  5. Tattoo day

    Show up rested, fed, and hydrated. Bring headphones if it helps you relax.

Cancellations & No-Shows

Please give at least 24 hours notice if you need to reschedule or cancel. Late cancellations and no-shows may forfeit the deposit. Full policy details available on request.
